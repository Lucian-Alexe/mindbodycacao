Mind

Psychotherapy & Mental Health Services

At the heart of our holistic approach to mental health is psychotherapy, where we draw from a variety of therapeutic methods to support you on your healing journey. Whether it's through traditional psychotherapy, EMDR, or Brainspotting, we're here to guide individuals through challenges like trauma, PTSD, anxiety, depression, and relationship struggles. We welcome both individuals and couples, including young adults, offering a safe and compassionate space for everyone. You can choose from remote or in-person consultations, whichever feels most comfortable for you.

EMDR is an effective therapy for healing trauma by reprocessing distressing memories and emotions. Using bilateral stimulation (typically eye movements), EMDR helps rewire the brain’s response to traumatic experiences, allowing clients to heal from PTSD, anxiety, and depression. This approach is highly effective for those who’ve experienced domestic abuse, childhood trauma, or complex PTSD.

Brainspotting is a powerful therapy for resolving emotional distress, trauma, and anxiety at its root. Through identifying specific “brain spots,” this technique accesses the areas of the brain where unresolved emotions and traumatic memories are stored, allowing for profound healing. Brainspotting is beneficial for clients dealing with trauma, chronic anxiety, depression, and emotional dysregulation.

Person-centered therapy creates a non-judgmental and empathetic environment where you are the expert of your own experience. This approach encourages self-discovery, personal growth, and the exploration of your inner world in a way that’s led by you. It’s ideal for clients seeking to deepen their understanding of themselves, build self-esteem, and improve emotional resilience.

Trauma-informed therapy ensures that we recognize the impact of past trauma on mental health and approach healing with empathy, safety, and trust. Whether you've experienced emotional abuse, domestic violence, or complex PTSD, our trauma-informed approach offers you a safe space to explore and process difficult emotions. We understand how trauma affects the brain and body, and we work with you to restore emotional balance and resilience.

Grief is a natural yet challenging process, and our therapist is here to guide you through the journey of loss, whether it’s the death of a loved one, the end of a relationship, or the emotional impact of life changes. Our approach to grief is holistic, offering a blend of psychotherapy, mindfulness techniques, and support to help you heal and integrate your loss into a new chapter of your life.

Existential therapy explores the deeper questions of life, such as meaning, identity, and purpose. This therapeutic approach is ideal for those undergoing life transitions, feeling existential anxiety, or struggling with a sense of purpose. Through existential therapy, we help you find meaning in your experiences, increase self-awareness, and take control of your future.

Motivational interviewing is a client-centered, directive therapy that helps individuals resolve ambivalence and develop motivation for change. Whether you’re struggling with addiction, relationship difficulties, or life changes, motivational interviewing helps clarify your goals and align your values with your actions, making it easier to create meaningful change.

Adjusting to life in a new country can be challenging, especially for expats living in Mexico. Cross-cultural adjustment therapy helps you navigate the complexities of adapting to a new culture, language, and environment. This therapy focuses on managing stress, overcoming feelings of isolation, and developing coping strategies to thrive in a foreign country, whether you're settling into Mexico or transitioning back to your home country.

LGBTQ+ affirmative therapy is a safe, supportive space for individuals of all sexual orientations and gender identities. We honor the unique challenges that LGBTQ+ individuals face, including identity exploration, societal pressures, and relationship struggles. Our therapist offers culturally sensitive support to help clients navigate these issues with confidence and self-acceptance.

Body

Movement & Embodiment Therapy

For the body, we focus on connecting the mind and body through movement therapy, yoga, and embodiment practices. The body holds onto stress, trauma, and emotional tension, and by engaging in mindful physical practices, we can unlock deep healing and emotional release. These therapies not only improve mental health but also promote physical well-being, helping you cultivate a balanced, resilient life.

Yoga therapy integrates the benefits of physical postures, breathwork, and mindfulness to support emotional healing. In combination with psychotherapy, yoga therapy helps release tension stored in the body, promoting deep relaxation, emotional regulation, and a greater sense of connection between the mind and body. It’s especially effective for stress management, trauma recovery, anxiety, and depression.

Movement therapy uses the body's natural rhythms and gestures to promote emotional healing and self-expression. By exploring movement and physical awareness, this therapy encourages individuals to connect with their inner emotions and release pent-up tension, promoting emotional balance and healing. Movement therapy is particularly beneficial for those dealing with trauma, body image issues, or emotional blockages.

Embodiment therapy focuses on connecting the mind and body to foster emotional healing. By becoming aware of body sensations and exploring how the body holds onto past trauma, clients can unlock emotional resilience and restore a sense of balance. Embodiment practices may include mindfulness, breathing exercises, somatic experiencing, and other body-based techniques to help release stored emotions, reduce stress, and increase self-awareness.

Somatic Experiencing is a therapeutic technique designed to help individuals process and release trauma stored in the body. This approach focuses on bodily sensations and works to gently release stored tension and trauma responses. It is especially effective for people with chronic trauma, PTSD, or those struggling with emotional or physical pain tied to unresolved experiences.

Dance therapy is a form of expressive therapy that uses movement to explore emotions, foster self-expression, and promote physical and emotional healing. Clients engage in spontaneous or structured movement as a way to connect with their emotions and physical sensations, supporting the release of tension, trauma, and stress. It is particularly useful for clients who find it difficult to express emotions verbally or have experienced body-related trauma.

Cacao Therapy

In addition to traditional psychotherapy, we offer Cacao Therapy as an optional, complementary practice to enhance emotional release and self-reflection. Cacao, a sacred plant medicine, has been used for centuries in healing rituals to open the heart, facilitate deep emotional processing, and promote spiritual insight. Through cacao ceremonies, you will experience a powerful journey of self-discovery, where you can release emotional blockages, connect with your inner peace, and cultivate clarity.

Cacao rituals are especially effective for individuals navigating grief, emotional trauma, or spiritual awakening. The heart-opening qualities of cacao help you process difficult emotions, gain new insights, and enhance your connection to yourself and the world around you. If you're seeking a more spiritual, integrative approach to healing, cacao therapy may be the perfect addition to your journey.
